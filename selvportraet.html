Self-portrait

1. Semester Assignment

Make a portrait of yourself

Use Adobe Illustrator to make a portrait of yourself using pentool

I chose the picture down below to make a portrait using pentool. I put it in illustrator and locked the picture and began using pentool. I started with the edges around my head, hair and shirt. Afterwards I began drawing the smaller details in my face, such as my nose, eyes and mouth and also the smallest details around nose and mouth. I then put color in and did a few shadows at the nose and on my neck.

This website is a presentation portfolio of my work as a multimedia design student at University College Lillebaelt. At this website you can see some of my work, learn a little about me and also contact me.
