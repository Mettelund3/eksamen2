Dating App

1. Semester Assignment

Design a dating app

Choose between designing a dating/playdate app for dogs, kids or elderly.

I chose to make a dating app/playdate app for dogs. I chose to make that because I have a dog myself and we have had some difficulties finding other dogs to have playdates with, and I have also heard that people call for a place where you can arrange playdates for your dog. I started the designing process with a thought of it being a little like tinder, because tinder is pretty intuitive and easy for everyone to figure out no matter what age and how well you know technology.

I started finding the pictures I wanted on each profile and every picture was taken by myself. I found 4 pictures for each 3 dogs I included and wrote a little about them in the context of searching for dogs to play with. I then performed user tests on 3 people with dogs, and I got some great feedback that I immediately adjusted. I had not included the owner's name at the first version of my app, and it got asked for so I included it on my second version.
