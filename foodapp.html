Food Experience App

1. Semester Assignment

Design a food ordering app

Interview one person and design a personal food ordering app from that interview

I started my assignment by interviewing a friend that often orders food. I interviewed her about her food ordering habits and what she would like in an app. She wanted to see what she ordered last time and be able to order that fast again as she usually orders the same. She also wants a page where she can order her favorites and she can mark her favorites too. She asked me if I could categorize the food into healthier, junk food and a middle way too.

I started the designing process on pinterest where I searched for app design and I got some inspiration for my own design. I started setting up the design elements in Adobe XD and found food pictures for my dishes I wanted to include that were copyright free. I then put my pictures in and added some buttons.
