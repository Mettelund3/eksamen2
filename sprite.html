Make a CSS animation

Use Adobe Illustrator to make a CSS animation

I started the process by making a storyboard on paper with a pencil. I wanted to see how it would evolve step by step if I put what I had in my head down on paper. Afterwards I made an illustrator document with the pixels I needed, in this case 16000px, to make 40 steps or different pictures in the same document with pictures on 400x400 pixels. I then put down the text I wanted, in this case - Mette Lund - and the 2 lines covering it up. I moved it 20 pixels every time, so it didnâ€™t move too little or too much each time.

After the design was finished in Adobe Illustrator I put it in my html file in a div tag, and did the design and keyframe work in CSS. I made it go for 4 seconds with my 40 steps.
